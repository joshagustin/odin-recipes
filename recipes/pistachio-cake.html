Pistachio Cake

Description

Scrumptious green tube cake made by enhancing a boxed cake mix with pistachio pudding and green food coloring

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 7 drops green food coloring
